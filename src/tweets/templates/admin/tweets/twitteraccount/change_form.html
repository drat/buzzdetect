Creating a token for a twitter account

  1. Open apps.twitter.com
  2. Click Create new app
  3. Fill the form and create a new app
  4. In the app detail page, click the "Keys and Access Tokens" tab
  5. At the bottom of the page, click "Create access token"
  6. In "Keys and Access tokens" tab, copy over access token, access token secret, consumer key and consumer secret
